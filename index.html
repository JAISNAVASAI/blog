SCIENCE LABS

Learning Science is made fun and experiential at Litera Valley Zee School, Hosur with fully equipped individual laboratories for Physics, Chemistry and Biology. All the laboratories are equipped with the latest technological devices, apparatus, kits for experiments, specimens, etc. in order to support cutting-edge experimentation.

THINK LAB

A dedicated period is allotted every week for all the students from Grade I and above for ‘Think Lab’. As the name implies, this is purely designed to kindle the fire for science in students with colourful and ready-to-use science kits. Individual kits are provided to the students based on the science concepts included in their respective syllabus. This exercise certainly redefines their aspiration providing hands-on experience and improving higher order thinking skills.

CS LABS

The school boasts of a well-equipped computer science lab with a complete set-up,housing latest desktop computers, that can accommodate and train more than hundred students at any given point of time. The systems, both arranged in circular hubs and individual cubicles, helps in peer learning and supports in handling concentrated individual tasks.

ROBOTICS LAB

Litera Valley Zee School, Hosur is the only school, perhaps in the whole World, to offer Robotics as part of the curriculum right from Std I. The school has associated with international names in furthering education, and has introduced Robotics in Association with Lego, USA and curricular tablet-based learning for the first time in South-India.

LANGUAGE LAB

Language is not a subject. This is the statement we abide by, as language learning is different from the way students learn other subjects. Developing a good command over the English language for every child on campus is one of the prime moto of the school as a person with great knowledge and zero effective communication skills is found to be absolute in today’s world. In order to develop our students’ vocabulary and to groom them as well-grounded personalities who not only excel in their field of specialization but also in having a compelling command over the English language, many special programs like Public Speaking, Spell Right, Pronounce Right and Speak Right are introduced. Through these programs, children develop their English language skills extensively.
